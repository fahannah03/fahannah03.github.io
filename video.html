Purpose: To create a video that promotes a feature offered at Millersville University. My group chose to promote the Tech Ed degree offered at Millersville University and centered our video around the various rooms around Osburn Hall (the Tech Ed building).

Story Board: A storyboard was crafted outlining the six scenes planned for the advertising video. This encompassed the Titles scene, four rooms within Osburn Hall, and the credit scene. It detailed the actions, lighting, and any dialogue that would occur in each room.

Script: Following the completion of the storyboard, our team proceeded to develop the script. This script contained the dialogue corresponding to each scene in the video. For each room, we explained its purpose and described the activities taking place within.

Final Video: In the final video, we included music in both the title and credit scenes. Additionally, we enhanced the voice-over for better audibility by editing it and removed background noise.
